## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Visit the NEXUS platform at [nexus-job-platform.vercel.app](https://nexus-job-platform.vercel.app)
2. Click "Get Started" or "Sign Up"
3. Choose your registration method:
   - **Email Registration:** Fill in your details
   - **Google Sign-In:** Click "Sign in with Google"
4. Complete the registration form
5. You're now ready to use NEXUS!

### Signing In

1. Click "Sign In" on the homepage
2. Enter your email and password, or use Google Sign-In
3. You'll be redirected to your dashboard

## For Job Seekers

### Dashboard Overview

Your dashboard shows:
- **Application Status:** Overview of your job applications
- **Quick Actions:** Fast access to key features
- **Recommended Jobs:** AI-suggested positions

### Uploading Your Resume

1. Go to **Profile** from the navigation menu
2. Scroll to the **Resume** section
3. Click **Upload Resume**
4. Select a PDF or Word document
5. Your resume is now ready for applications!

**Tips:**
- Keep your resume under 5MB
- PDF format works best
- You can upload multiple resumes

### Browsing Jobs

1. Click **Browse Jobs** in the navigation
2. Use filters to narrow your search:
   - Job Type (Full-time, Part-time, Contract, Internship)
   - Location Type (On-site, Remote, Hybrid)
   - Experience Level (Entry, Mid, Senior, Lead)
3. Click on a job card to view details

### Applying for a Job

1. Click on a job to view details
2. Click **Apply Now**
3. Select your resume
4. Choose cover letter option:
   - **No Cover Letter:** Skip this step
   - **AI Generate:** Let AI create one for you
   - **Write Your Own:** Enter custom text
5. Click **Submit Application**

### Tracking Applications

1. Go to **My Applications** from the navigation
2. View all your submitted applications
3. Check status for each:
   - **Pending:** Application received
   - **Reviewed:** Employer has seen your application
   - **Shortlisted:** You're being considered
   - **Interview:** Interview scheduled
   - **Offered:** Job offer extended
   - **Rejected:** Not selected

## For Administrators

### Accessing Admin Dashboard

1. Sign in with an admin account
2. You'll automatically see the Admin Dashboard
3. Navigate using the tabs:
   - **Dashboard:** Overview statistics
   - **Manage Jobs:** Create and edit job listings
   - **Applications:** Review applicant submissions
   - **Users:** View registered users

### Creating a New Job

1. Go to **Manage Jobs** tab
2. Click **Post New Job**
3. Fill in the job details:
   - Job Title
   - Company Name
   - Location
   - Job Type
   - Description
   - Requirements
   - Skills
   - Salary (optional)
4. Click **Post Job**

### Managing Job Listings

- **Edit:** Click the edit icon to modify a job
- **Activate/Deactivate:** Toggle job visibility
- **Delete:** Remove a job permanently

### Reviewing Applications

1. Go to **Applications** tab
2. Click **View** on any application
3. Review applicant details:
   - Profile information
   - Resume (downloadable)
   - Cover letter
4. Update status using the dropdown menu

### Application Status Guide

| Status | When to Use |
|--------|-------------|
| Pending | New application, not yet reviewed |
| Reviewed | You've looked at the application |
| Shortlisted | Candidate is being considered |
| Interview | Interview has been scheduled |
| Offered | Job offer has been made |
| Rejected | Candidate not selected |

## AI Features

### AI Job Match

Get personalized job recommendations based on your resume.

1. Go to **AI Match** from navigation
2. Choose your resume or paste resume text
3. Enter the job description
4. Click **Analyze Match**
5. View your results:
   - **Match Score:** How well you fit (0-100%)
   - **Strengths:** What makes you a good fit
   - **Gaps:** Areas for improvement
   - **Recommendations:** Next steps

### AI Cover Letter Generator

Create tailored cover letters instantly.

1. When applying for a job, select **AI Generate**
2. The AI analyzes:
   - Your resume
   - The job description
   - Company information
3. A personalized cover letter is generated
4. Review and edit if needed
5. Submit with your application

### AI Career Assistant (Chatbot)

Get instant career advice and platform help.

1. Click the chat icon (bottom-right corner)
2. Ask questions like:
   - "How do I improve my resume?"
   - "What skills are in demand for data science?"
   - "Help me prepare for an interview"
3. Get instant, personalized responses

### Updating Your Profile

1. Go to **Profile** from navigation

   - Skills

## FAQs

### General Questions

**Q: Is NEXUS free to use?**
A: Yes, NEXUS is completely free for job seekers.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Contact support to request account deletion.

**Q: Can I apply to the same job twice?**
A: No, each user can only submit one application per job.

### Technical Questions

**Q: What file formats are accepted for resumes?**
A: PDF, DOC, and DOCX files up to 5MB.

**Q: Why can't I see my uploaded resume?**
A: Try refreshing the page or re-uploading the file.

**Q: The AI features aren't working. What should I do?**
A: Wait a few minutes and try again. If the issue persists, contact support.

### Application Questions

**Q: Can I edit my application after submitting?**
A: No, applications cannot be modified after submission.

**Q: How long before I hear back from employers?**
A: Response times vary by company. Check your application status regularly.

**Q: What does each application status mean?**
A: See the [Application Status Guide](#application-status-guide) above.
